🏘️ Welcome to PixelVille

A living sandbox where you drop the buildings and the village figures out the rest.

  • 🏠 Drop a house — a road appears automatically and a family moves in, car and all.
  • 🏪 Drop a shop, office or school — roads connect themselves, people get jobs, kids enroll, and everyone replans their day.
  • 🌄 Drop mountains, rivers & lakes — roads carve around rock and bridge over water. Watch for hikers on the peaks… and mysterious lights up there at night.
  • 🍂 Seasons & weather — spring blossoms, summer fireflies, autumn leaves, winter snow. Rain pours, storms flash, and heavy rain leaves puddles.
  • 🌙 Real nights — glowing windows, street lamps, car headlights, torches.
  • 🚨 Crime & justice — unemployment breeds burglars. Build police stations (one per district!), a courthouse and a prison-worthy justice system.
  • 📖 Task book — every project, emergency and election is logged and crossed off when done. Click the book in the top bar.
  • 🚌 Transport — bus stops, taxis, railways with trains, docks with boats & ferries, and helicopters between helipads.
  • 💍 Generations — villagers fall in love, marry, raise kids, get promoted, grow old and pass the village on.
  • 🏦 Real economy — land is bought from the government (prices vary by district), banks give loans, businesses boom or go under.

Controls: click a palette item, then click the map to place · drag map or WASD to pan · wheel to zoom · right-click / Esc to cancel a tool · click any building to inspect it · space pauses · keys 1–4 set game speed · H hides the sidebar for full-screen viewing.
